Grave Robber website and app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when visitors use the Grave Robber website at graverobberpunk.com and the Grave Robber mobile app, how that information is used, and what choices visitors have.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when visitors use the website or app:

  • Daily visitor information, including IP address, browser or device user agent, first seen time, and last seen time.
  • Contact or signup information submitted through forms, such as name, email address, phone number, zip code, and message content.
  • Graffiti Wall submissions, including fan name, optional email address, message text, uploaded photo, and artwork created with the paint tool.
  • Uploaded media files, such as photos, drawings, or other images submitted through public features or managed by site administrators.
  • Basic technical information that may be received automatically by hosting, database, media hosting, form, payment, social media, and widget providers.

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Count daily home page visitors.
  • Respond to contact, booking, merch, press, signup, or general messages.
  • Review, approve, hide, or delete Graffiti Wall posts before they appear publicly.
  • Display approved fan posts, photos, and artwork on the Graffiti Wall.
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the website, app, admin panel, forms, uploads, and media features.

Daily Visitor IP Address Storage

The website records IP addresses and user agents for the home page visitor counter. These records are grouped by day and are used to count unique daily visitors. Visitor IP records older than the current day are deleted by the system when the visitor counter runs, so this daily tracking is intended to reset each day.

Public Graffiti Wall Posts

Graffiti Wall submissions are not posted publicly automatically. They are stored for admin review. If approved, the fan name, message, uploaded photo, artwork, and submission date may appear publicly on the website and in the app. Email addresses submitted with Graffiti Wall posts are used for administration and are not shown publicly by the public Graffiti Wall display.

Contact, Signup, and Email Information

If visitors submit a contact form, signup form, or email message, the information provided may be stored and used to reply, manage band communication, provide updates, or handle booking and fan requests. This can include name, email address, phone number, zip code, and the contents of the message.

Photos, Artwork, and Uploads

Photos, fan-submitted artwork, and other uploaded images may be stored with a media hosting provider and may remain stored until removed by an administrator. Visitors should only upload photos or artwork they have the right to submit and should not upload private information they do not want reviewed by the site administrator.

Third-Party Services

The website and app may use third-party services to operate features, including hosting, database storage, media uploads, forms, show widgets, social media links, and payment links. These may include services such as Railway, Cloudinary, Google Forms, Bandsintown, PayPal, Facebook, Instagram, Spotify, TikTok, X, Threads, and YouTube. When visitors use or open those services, the third party may collect information under its own privacy policy.

Sharing Information

We do not sell visitor personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that help operate the website, app, forms, uploads, payments, hosting, database, analytics-like visitor counting, and public display features. Information may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the website, app, band, visitors, or others.

Retention

Daily visitor IP records are intended to be deleted after the current day as described above. Contact messages, signup submissions, Graffiti Wall submissions, uploaded photos, artwork, and admin-managed content may be kept until they are no longer needed or until an administrator deletes them.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect stored information, but no website, app, database, upload service, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children

The website and app are intended for general band, music, and fan community use. They are not directed to children under 13. Children should not submit personal information without permission from a parent or guardian.

Privacy Requests

Visitors may request deletion or correction of information they submitted by contacting Grave Robber at graverobber.punk@gmail.com. Please include enough detail to identify the submission or message.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new last updated date.
